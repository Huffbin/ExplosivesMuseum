ARTILLERY

Artillery has played a major role in military history for centuries. It consists of large-caliber systems designed to project munitions over long distances in support of ground operations.

Overview

This gallery explores the history, development and engineering of artillery systems throughout history.

Typical Characteristics

  • Long-range indirect fire capability
  • Large-caliber projectiles
  • Towed or self-propelled designs
  • Used in many historical conflicts

Engineering

Modern artillery combines mechanical engineering, recoil management systems, precision aiming equipment and durable construction to operate effectively under demanding conditions.
